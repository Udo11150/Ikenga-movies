The Conjuring Review - Still the Scariest Ghost Movie in 2026?

The Conjuring follows paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren as they help a family terrorized by a dark presence in their farmhouse. What makes it terrifying is that it’s “based on a true story”.

Director James Wan builds tension with silence instead of cheap jump scares. Every creak, every shadow feels dangerous. My rating: 9/10. If you only watch one haunted house movie, make it this one. The atmosphere stays with you long after credits roll.

Watch tip: The 4K Blu-ray has amazing sound for horror nights at home.
